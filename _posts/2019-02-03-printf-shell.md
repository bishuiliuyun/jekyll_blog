### printf

```shell
printf "%5s %10s %10s\\n" No Name Mark
printf "%5s %10s %10.2f\\n" 1 Sarath 80.3456
printf "%5s %10s %10.2f\\n" 2 James 90.9989
printf "%5s %10s %10.2f\\n" 3 Jeff 98.7

printf "\\n\\n\\n\\n"

printf "%-5s %-10s %-10s\\n" No Name Mark
printf "%-5s %-10s %-10.2f\\n" 1 Sarath 80.3456
printf "%-5s %-10s %-10.2f\\n" 2 James 90.9989
printf "%-5s %-10s %-10.2f\\n" 3 Jeff 98.7
```
